About us

Lizmotors Mobility is a technology start-up focused on the IoT and Mobility space. Our mission is to build a connected ecosystem that can improve personal safety, increase productivity, and help businesses manage their operation more efficiently. Lizmotors offers products and services that unlock value for end-users. Our solution provides a better understanding of customers that helps companies tailor products, develop new services, and increase customer loyalty, broadening channels and access to new customers using a data-driven approach, optimization of pricing across products and services.
